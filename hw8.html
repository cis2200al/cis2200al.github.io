In-Class & Homework Exercises

Due Date: Monday, November 6, 11:59 pm

Individual Assignment

  • Question 1: What are some computer hardware that you can find connected to the outside of a computer?
  • Question 2: Think back to the chapter on Moore’s Law, what have we learned about one kind of computing hardware?
  • Question 3: Discuss one programming language that is commonly used in your field or that you would like to learn/improve on/are currently learning. (a) Is it general purpose or domain specific? Explain. (b) Is it compiled or interpreted, or can it be either? Explain. (c) Is it object-oriented, procedural, or functional, or any other category? Explain.

Group Assignment

  • Question 1: Research and describe the major differences between Windows vs. Unix/Linux, or between Android vs. iOS. • Question 2: What are the implications of these differences for users and organizations?
