Week 6 Wiki Assignment

There are several requirements that I have added and/or updated in the assignment. Please check here and go to Blackboard to post your submission or updated it (if you have already submitted).

The point of this assignment is to faciliate your review process for the midterm. You not only learn and understand the concept that you write about, but also can learn the other concepts that the other classmates write about. You can also learn from the edits of the other classmates for the concept that you originally create an entry for.

You can also comment on the wiki pages, regarding your questions, concerns, if you have any.
