Online Assignment: Wiki Page on Blackboard

Due Date: Sunday, Oct 22, 11:59 pm

Instructions

  • 1 Go to Blackboard page of the course
  • 2 Click on "Course Tools" [left side panel]
  • 3 Click on "Wikis" [look toward the right side of the page]
  • 4 Click on "Midterm Review"
  • 5 Read requirements
  • 6 Click on "Create Wiki Page" on top left corner to post your wiki entry
  • 7 Click on "Edit Wiki Content" on the wiki page not created by you to add content according to requirements below.

Requirements

  • 1 Find one concept listed at the end of the textbook chapters below.
  • 2 Create one wiki entry [Click on Create Wiki Page on top left corner] for the concept that you have chosen.
  • 3 In the wiki that you create, write down the definition for the concept in your own words, and find two real-life examples that help you understand and remember the concept clearly, either found in the textbook (if found in textbook, describe the examples in your own words), or on the Internet. Cite all sources (even the textbook).
  • 4 Edit one wiki entry that is not created by you to add one more real-life example that helps you understand and remember the concept clearly, either found in the textbook (if found in textbook, describe the examples in your own words), or on the Internet. Again, cite all sources even the textbook.

Chapters to Review for Midterm

Chapter 5 Moore's Law

Chapter 6 Disruptive Technologies

Chapter 16 Internet & Telecommunications

Chapter 17 Information Security
