New Site, Week 3 & Group Formation

Posted on Wed 20 September 2017 by Anh Luong

Posted in announcements

Hi everyone,

Just want to give you an overview of what you need to know and complete this week.

New Site

Syllabus & Schedule You can find the Syllabus and Tentative Schedule Outline by clicking on "Syllabus" and "Schedule" on the top of the site. The schedule is not fixed, and there might be some minor changes in the content we cover in some weeks. I will send out announcements in advance if such changes occur. Similarly, in the case that the Syllabus needs changes in some details as the semester progresses, I will inform you of them in the syllabus.

Assignments For each week, I will post the assignments' requirements here, you can click on "Assignments" at the top of the site, and find the assignments according to the weeks there.

Materials This page provides you with what you need to learn each week - lecture slides, readings (textbook &/or articles), and videos.

It is important and required that you always check the pages "Announcements", "Materials", and "Assignments" for each week.

Resources This page is meant to be a convenient hub of important links, some of which you are required to use for the readings and assignments of the class (blacboard, textbook, google drive), some of which are optional and serve as additional resources that can benefit your studying.

Week 3 Requirements

To access lecture slides, find out about the required readings, and video watching for week 3, go to Materials > Week 3 Materials

To view week 3 assignments, go to Assignments > Week 3 Assignments

NOTE: to submit your homework, email to this address: athluong@gmail.com with subject "Week 3 Assignment"

Group Formation

In the following weeks, there will be some weeks that I will assign homework exercises for groups. Additionally, there will be group projects (Excel & Access).

Thus, it is important that you form groups of 3 members as soon as possible (preferably by mid-week next week, so you guys can complete the written assignments in groups for next week). Since our class has 61 students, one group will have 4 members.

I will create a google spreadsheet and you can enter your names according to the groups that you have formed. The link to the google spreadsheet will be posted in the page "Resources".

Let me know if you have any questions.

Best,

Anh Luong
