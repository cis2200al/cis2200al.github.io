Posted on Mon 25 September 2017 by Anh Luong

Posted in assignments

PollEverywhere Questions on Moore's Law & Disruptive Technologies

Due Date:

Sunday, Oct 1, 11:59 PM

Requirements:

Although we did not meet in-class for this week, the in-class participation grade for this week will still be assessed by a series of questions on PollEverywhere like usual.

Please go to https://pollev.com/anhluong, log into your Polleverywhere account and start answering the survey titled "Moore's Law & Disruptive Technologies".

You are only allowed to answer once for each question, so think carefully before entering your answer.

Quizzes 3 & 4

Due Date:

Sunday, Oct 1, 11:59 pm

Details:

Quiz 3 tests your knowledge on concepts and issues related to Moore's Law and Disruptive Technologies. Quiz 4 tests your knowledge on basic concepts related to Internet and Telecommunications.

To take the quizzes: Go to the Blackboard page of the class, click on Course Documents and then "Online Quiz 3", and "Online Quiz 4".

You are only allowed 1 attempt for the quizzes.

Internet & Telecommunication Exercise

Due Date:

Sunday, October 1, 11:59 pm

Objective:

To understand the basics of the Internet & Telecommunications.

Requirements:

Complete the set of Multiple Choice Questions posted on Blackboard, in the section "Course Documents", named "Week 4 Homework Questions".

You are allowed 3 attempts for these questions.
